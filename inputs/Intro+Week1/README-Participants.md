## Calendar convention — virtual weeks vs physical dates

Throughout all participant docs, you will see entries in the form:

> **Fri 17.04.2026 ("Monday, Week 1")**

The **physical date** on the left is when the event actually happens in real life. The **virtual day in brackets** tells you where you are within the 5-day week structure. Week 1 has 7 working days aligned to a 5-day virtual week (stretched for breathing room). Weeks 2–5 normally start on a physical Tuesday and shift further due to public holidays on **1 May** and **14 May**. Your coach team confirms the exact physical date for each virtual day in the **Teams General channel** at the start of every week. Full explanation is in `README-Participants-Intro-Week1.md`.

## Rubrics — sealed until the gate begins

The specific rubric (criteria, weights, and pass threshold) for each gate is sealed until the start of that gate exercise, to keep you focused on doing the work well rather than optimising a numeric weight table. The **Capstone rubric** is the one exception — it is shared at the start of Virtual Monday of Week 5, because the 4-day format makes it valuable to have the criteria visible while you design and build. Deliverable lists in each weekly file tell you exactly what to submit.
